ABOUT TRIKOD

Trikod is a Cancer Prediction Website which analysis the datasets of patient's diagnosis and predicts the future scope of Cancer in its every branch. It also visualizes the prediction which helps in understanding the behaviour of the pattern in a better way.

The objectives of Trikod are as follows:

(1) It predict the possibility of the incidence using the person's medical history. This may help us to identify and warn the respective person or alert them to take certin precautions before time.

(2) The risk factor of Cancer can be assessed using Machine Learning Algorithms to plan and decide the best policy for the individual.

(3) It gives a brief and specific idea about the type of Cancer that has been the reason behind the deaths of majority. The concerned branch of Cancer is then taken under research to find cure.
